History

Rapids Rafting Co. started with a raft, a river, and a wild idea: "Turn weekend thrills into everyday adventures." Since then, we’ve been riding rapids, dodging rocks, and high-fiving paddlers from all walks of life. We believe laughter is best served soaking wet.
